# Join the Group

We are always looking for motivated students and postdocs who are excited about computational chemistry and catalysis.

## Prospective PhD Students

If you are interested in joining as a PhD student, please:

1. Apply to the [Georgia Tech ChBE PhD program](https://chbe.gatech.edu/graduate-admissions) and mention the Kreitz Research Group in your application
2. Once you are accepted, you can contact Prof. Kreitz to discuss available projects. 

Prof. Kreitz does not have any influence on the admission process and will not respond to emails prior to admission. 

## Prospective Postdocs

The Kreitz group does not currently have any funding for postdoctoral scholars. If you are interested in joining and have independent funding, please contact Prof. Kreitz. Due to bandwidth, Prof. Kreitz may not respond to unsolicited emails inquiring about positions that are not available.

For PhD students from Germany, who are interested in joining the Kreitz group as a postdoc: Prof. Kreitz was a Feodor Lynen Postdoctoral Research Fellow from the Alexander von Humboldt Foundation and is able to host Feodor Lynen Postdoctoral Researchers. If you are interested in applying for such a fellowship to join the group, contact Prof. Kreitz.
